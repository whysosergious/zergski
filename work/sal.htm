Mobile single & multiplayer highscore chaser

2019

After I completed a six month course in programming with C#. I wanted to test my newly aquired skills. Deciding that Unity was a somewhat overkill for this type of project I went with the Godot enginge.

Non-existant documentation

While I quickly realized that Godots support for C# was fairly new, and documentation nearly non existant. I stubbornly refused to give up and pushed on, by translating documentation from a different coding language. Which on the bright side gave me more insight into C# and the engine.

The engine barely added support for my chosen language. And thus all of it's documentation was in the GDScript language more similar to Python. Slowly but surely I managed to accoplish my planned endevours and even helped a couple of poor fellas in the community facing similar problems.
