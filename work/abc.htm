aptBit Content Manager

____

By developing a simple CRUD interface, implemented in all my freelance work, I was able to give my clients a way to manage their online presense without the need for constant maintnance of plugins and system updates.

Through experience and extensive canvas.

Working and conversing with a large number of owners and employees of smaller (and some larger) businesses, one question was often asked.

- How much control is actually required?

The answere is a simply "not a lot". Business hours, price list together with a menu of products and/or services and a way post and edit news in a blog-like fashion. With a small variation of features depending on the business. But all are in need of a system that simply works.

Later versions make use of ES6+ features like, to just to name a couple, asynchronous processing for, well .. asynchronous processing, loading of external scripts etc. Classes and class inheritance for better handling of data as well as utilize Node.js and its f/s module to create a file-based-SQL database hybrid, strengthening security without sacrificing performance.
