Brewers Association

2019

Representing the largest association of american brewers and breweries at the Stockholm Beer and Whisky Festival. And acting as the managing exchibitor. I hired and lead my own team, wrote and designed the menu, managed inventory, schedual, economy and other duties.

Printed and digital

Designing the printed menu, knowing the crowd of people often present. I created a simple mobile version available on the web. Accessed through a short url of the scal of a QR code I took advantage of the digital format by giving the possiblity to sort the various beers by style.
